Deepika Nursery is the brainchild of Hoshiyar Singh Jharjharia. He hails from farming community of Solana Village. He is a avid plant lover and a man with enterprenural spirit. In year 2009, he started the Nursey by setting aside a sizable part of his cultivation land.

All the nursery siblings are nurtured with fresh water from borewell and quality inhouse khad. Khad is made by fertilizing cattle dung with earth worms. In the starting year, approximately two lakhs plants were prepared and a sizable part of it was sold. People from nearby villages and towns came to purchase the plants. Many tenders were also serviced for wholesale dealers in cities during rainy season. Seeing the quality of plants from nursery and need to promote plantation, Govt of Rajasthan has placed a tender to provide the plants to them.


It feels great that Deepika Nursery is playing the role of catalyst to increase tree plantation. It gives satisfaction and spreads smiles on everybody's face.
